Forms

This section demonstrates clean, responsive form layouts using Angular Material components styled with pure CSS. The form below showcases proper spacing, alignment, and organization of form fields with a modern design that follows Material Design principles.

The errors are handled with a custom directive appFieldError that automatically detects the validations applied to the form fields and adds the error to the mat-error element.
